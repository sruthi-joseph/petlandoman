About Us

Experiencing the Premium Standard of Pet Love & Care in the Sultanate of Oman

Who We Are

Our mission is simple:

To provide complete pet care solutions under one roof while creating memorable experiences for pets and their families.

We understand that every pet has unique needs. Therefore, we carefully select high-quality products and offer professional services that prioritize the health, comfort, and happiness of every pet.

Today, Petland proudly serves thousands of customers across 11 convenient branches in the Sultanate of Oman (including our Petland and Pethouse outlets) and continues to expand its commitment to excellence and innovation in pet care.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred pet care brand in the Middle East by providing exceptional products, professional services, and outstanding customer experiences.

Our Mission

  • Deliver premium quality pet products
  • Promote responsible pet ownership
  • Provide exceptional pet care services
  • Ensure pet health, safety, and happiness
  • Build lasting relationships with pet families

Our Values

Love

We treat every pet with kindness, compassion, and respect.

Innovation
